Sweet Potato Casserole Dessert

This sweet potato casserole has a crunchy pecan streusel topping. This delicious recipe was given to me by my brother-in-law.

Prep Time: Cook Time:
20 mins 25 mins
Total Time: Servings:
45 mins 16 person

Ingredients

  • 4 ½ cups cooked and mashed sweet potatoes
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • ½ cup butter, melted
  • ⅓ cup milk
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup light brown sugar
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • ⅓ cup butter
  • 1 cup chopped pecans

Directions

Step1

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.

Step2

Mix mashed sweet potatoes, sugar, 1/2 cup melted butter, milk, eggs, and vanilla extract together in a large bowl; spread into the prepared baking dish.

Step3

Mix brown sugar and flour together in a small bowl; cut in 1/3 cup butter until mixture is crumbly, then stir in pecans. Sprinkle streusel mixture over sweet potatoes.

Step4

Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 25 minutes.
